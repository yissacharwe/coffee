Coffee

My first cup of coffee was probably at age 14. I was curious, so I tried

  • Espresso
  • Cappucino
  • Dark

and many more..

I started with two or three spoons of sugar and over the years gradually decreased the amount to no sugar at all. The amount of coffee was increasing and reached a peak of 6 to 8 cups a day. Today I drink 4 to 6 cups and I am happy about it.
